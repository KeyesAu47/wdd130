Our Trips

River Rapids Adventure

Experience the thrill of Class III-IV rapids with expert guides. This trip is perfect for those seeking excitement and a challenge. You'll navigate through breathtaking scenery and enjoy the adrenaline rush of the rapids!

Forest Scenic Journey

A peaceful rafting experience with scenic views of the surrounding forest. This trip is suitable for beginners and families, offering a relaxing day on the water while enjoying nature's beauty.

Embark on a challenging adventure through rugged canyons. This trip is designed for advanced rafters, providing an exhilarating experience with demanding rapids and stunning canyon vistas.
